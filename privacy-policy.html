Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TournaBlade ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the mobile application provided by GoldDev ("we", "us", or "our").

TournaBlade is designed to help users create and manage local tournaments and events.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • User ID associated with your account
  • Profile nickname or username
  • Tournament and event data you create or manage
  • Participation and role data related to tournaments or events
  • Private match chat messages sent inside the App
  • Basic app usage and technical diagnostics, if enabled through our service providers

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide tournament and event features
  • Enable participation, roles, standings, brackets, and related flows
  • Enable private match chat where available
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and functionality
  • Fix bugs and improve the product

3. Authentication and Guest Use

Some features may be available without a full registered account, such as limited guest flows for local tournaments. Other features, especially account-based event features, require a stable registered account.

4. Third-Party Services

TournaBlade uses third-party services to operate the App. These providers may process data on our behalf according to their own privacy policies.

  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Expo: https://expo.dev/privacy

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may use technical analytics or diagnostics tools to understand app stability, feature usage, and overall product performance. We do not use your data for third-party advertising tracking.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the App, maintain the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

Tournament, profile, and related account data may remain stored until you delete your account or request deletion where applicable.

7. Account Deletion

TournaBlade provides in-app account deletion. When you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize relevant personal data associated with that account, unless we must retain certain information for legal, security, or legitimate operational reasons.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App
  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect rights, safety, security, or prevent fraud and abuse

9. Children’s Privacy

TournaBlade is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and remove the data if appropriate.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information processed through the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, deletion, or objection to certain processing. You can contact us using the email below for privacy-related requests.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date.
